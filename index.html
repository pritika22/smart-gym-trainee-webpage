PRO TIPS

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 3L of water daily. Dehydration reduces strength and focus by up to 20%.

😴

Sleep 7–9 Hours

Muscles grow during rest, not during workouts. Prioritize sleep for maximum gains.

🥩

Protein is Key

Aim for 1.6–2g of protein per kg of body weight daily to support muscle repair.

🔥

Always Warm Up

Spend 5–10 minutes on dynamic stretching before training to prevent injuries.

📈

Progressive Overload

Increase weight or reps each week. Your muscles grow when challenged beyond comfort.

🧠

Mind-Muscle Connection

Focus on the muscle you're training. Slow, controlled reps beat heavy, sloppy ones.
